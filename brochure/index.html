The Challenge

Every tea estate loses money to problems it cannot see.

Manual systems create gaps. Gaps create losses. Most estates don't realize how much they leak through attendance fraud, weight errors, and delayed decisions - every single day.

Proxy Attendance

Workers punch in for absent colleagues. Paper registers have zero verification. You pay for people who aren't working - and you'll never know the real number.

Weight Disputes

Plucking weights recorded by hand, copied into another register, then typed into a computer. Every handoff is a chance for error, manipulation, or delay.

Blind Decisions

Production and labor data arrives late - sometimes the next day. You manage a live operation on yesterday's numbers. Opportunities and problems go unnoticed.

Disconnected Records

Attendance in one place. Weighing in another. Payroll in a third. No single view of what actually happened today. Reconciliation burns hours weekly.

GardenSuite eliminates all four problems.

One connected system replaces paper registers, manual weighing, separate payroll software, and delayed reporting with a single, verified chain of data - from the garden gate to your phone screen.

What Makes Us Different

Two technologies no other tea ERP offers.

Breakthrough 1

Face Recognition Attendance

Every worker verified by their face at the garden gate. No proxy punching. No buddy punching. No paper registers. The verified identity follows every record - from attendance to kamjari to payroll.

Breakthrough 2

Smart Wireless Scale

Tea leaf weight captured wirelessly and linked to the verified worker instantly. No manual notebooks. No copying errors. No disputes. One weigh, one record - weight data flows directly into processing and payroll.

Works Fully Offline

No internet at the garden? No problem. Full operation runs offline. Data syncs automatically when connected.

Zero Manual Entry

Face verified, weight captured, record created. No handwriting, no re-typing, no room for manipulation or mistakes.

One Verified Chain

Identity, attendance, weight, kamjari, payroll - every step linked to a real, verified worker. End-to-end accuracy.

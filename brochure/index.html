The Challenge

Tea gardens lose money to invisible problems.

Every day, estates face attendance fraud, weight mismatches, delayed reports, and disconnected records across departments. These problems compound silently.

👤

Proxy Attendance

Workers punch in for others. Manual registers have no verification. Payroll inflates with ghost entries no one catches until audit.

⚖️

Weight Mismatches

Field weighing disconnected from worker records. Manual entry creates errors, disputes, and delayed kamjari processing.

📊

Late Daily Reports

Management waits until evening or next day for basic production numbers. Decisions happen on stale, incomplete data.

🔗

Disconnected Systems

Attendance in one register, weighing in another, payroll in a third. No single view of operations. Reconciliation takes hours.

GardenSuite solves all four.

One connected platform replaces fragmented tools with a clear flow from field attendance to management dashboard.

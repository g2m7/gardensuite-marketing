The Challenge

Tea estate attendance is still slow, manual, and hard to verify.

Manual registers delay reporting. Field attendance and weighment sit in separate processes. Management gets delayed visibility. Small estates want control, but not a heavy software commitment.

Attendance Disputes

Manual registers have zero verification. Workers can punch in for absent colleagues. You pay for people who aren't working and you'll never know the real number.

Separate Processes

Attendance is recorded one way, weighing another. They never connect. Reconciliation takes hours and errors slip through every day.

Delayed Visibility

Production and labour data arrives late. Sometimes the next day. You manage a live operation on yesterday's numbers. Problems go unnoticed.

Heavy Software Fear

Small estates want control but don't want a full ERP commitment. They need something simple that works, not a system that takes months to learn.

A simple attendance system built for tea estates.

Facial attendance capture on mobile. Weight capture through the paired setup. Daily visibility through MIS dashboard. Designed for estates that want a fast operational start - without committing to a full ERP rollout.

How it works.

Face Attendance

Capture attendance with facial recognition.

Every worker verified by their face. No proxy punching. No buddy punching. No paper registers. The verified identity follows every record - from attendance to weight to the MIS dashboard.

Weight Capture

Record weight through paired device workflow.

Plucking weight linked to the verified worker instantly. No manual notebooks. No copying errors. One weigh, one record. Data flows directly into the daily dashboard for management visibility.

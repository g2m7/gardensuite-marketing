## 1) Brochure Goal

- Show tea estate owners and managers that GardenSuite is a complete, practical, and trusted tea garden ERP.
- Explain the biggest pain points and how GardenSuite solves them.
- Build trust with company history and client list.
- Push one clear action: book a demo.

## 2) Target Audience

- Tea estate owners
- Garden managers
- Factory managers

## 3) Core Message

GardenSuite is a complete tea garden management ERP that handles attendance, plucking weight, payroll, factory, stores, accounts, and reporting in one system.

## 4) Key Differentiators

1. Face recognition attendance to stop proxy punching.
2. Smart wireless scale linked with worker identity and plucking weight.
3. Offline garden operations with cloud dashboard access.
4. MIS web dashboard for daily visibility on phone, tablet, or laptop.
